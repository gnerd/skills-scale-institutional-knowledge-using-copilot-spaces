# Role Assignment and Onboarding Checklist

This checklist ensures consistent role assignment and effective onboarding for project team members at OctoAcme.

---

## Purpose

Provide a structured approach to assigning roles and onboarding team members to ensure clarity, accountability, and quick ramp-up on projects.

---

## Role Assignment Checklist

### Pre-Assignment

- [ ] **Identify required roles**: Review project scope to determine all necessary roles (see [Roles & Personas](octoacme-roles-and-personas.md))
- [ ] **Assess availability**: Confirm candidate availability for the project duration
- [ ] **Evaluate skills fit**: Match candidate skills and experience to role requirements
- [ ] **Clarify reporting structure**: Define who the role reports to and key collaborators
- [ ] **Document time allocation**: Specify expected time commitment (full-time, partial, advisory)

### Assignment Confirmation

- [ ] **Role assignment documented**: Record role assignment in project charter or team roster
- [ ] **Communicate assignment**: Notify the team member and stakeholders of the assignment
- [ ] **Access granted**: Ensure appropriate access to project tools, repositories, and documentation
- [ ] **Calendar invites sent**: Add team member to relevant meetings and ceremonies
- [ ] **Responsibility handoff**: If replacing someone, schedule knowledge transfer session

---

## Onboarding Checklist by Role

### All Roles

- [ ] **Project overview briefing**: Review project charter, goals, and success metrics
- [ ] **Team introductions**: Meet key team members and understand their roles
- [ ] **Documentation walkthrough**: Review project management docs (see [README](README.md))
- [ ] **Tools setup**: Confirm access to communication, project management, and development tools
- [ ] **Process orientation**: Understand team rituals (standups, planning, retrospectives)
- [ ] **Escalation paths**: Review escalation procedures (Team → PM → Product Lead → Sponsor)
- [ ] **First week goals**: Establish clear objectives for the first week

### Project Sponsor Onboarding

- [ ] **Strategic context briefing**: Review business case, ROI expectations, and strategic alignment
- [ ] **Budget overview**: Understand project budget, funding sources, and approval thresholds
- [ ] **Stakeholder map**: Review key stakeholders and their interests
- [ ] **Reporting cadence**: Establish preferred frequency and format for status updates
- [ ] **Escalation expectations**: Clarify when and how issues should be escalated

### Business Analyst Onboarding

- [ ] **Requirements repository**: Access existing requirements documentation and templates
- [ ] **Stakeholder directory**: Identify key stakeholders for requirements gathering
- [ ] **Domain knowledge transfer**: Schedule sessions with subject matter experts
- [ ] **Backlog orientation**: Review current backlog and upcoming refinement sessions
- [ ] **Acceptance criteria standards**: Understand format and quality expectations

### Communications Lead Onboarding

- [ ] **Communication plan review**: Review existing communication plan and templates
- [ ] **Stakeholder groups**: Understand different stakeholder groups and their information needs
- [ ] **Brand/messaging guidelines**: Review any organizational messaging standards
- [ ] **Distribution channels**: Familiarize with communication tools and distribution lists
- [ ] **Announcement schedule**: Review upcoming milestones requiring communication

### Change Manager Onboarding

- [ ] **Change impact assessment**: Review scope of changes and impacted groups
- [ ] **Training resources**: Inventory existing training materials and platforms
- [ ] **Resistance factors**: Understand potential adoption challenges
- [ ] **Success metrics**: Define how change adoption will be measured
- [ ] **Support channels**: Establish how users can get help during transition

### Quality Assurance Lead Onboarding

- [ ] **Testing infrastructure**: Access test environments, tools, and frameworks
- [ ] **Quality standards**: Review existing quality standards and metrics
- [ ] **Test documentation**: Access test plans, test cases, and defect tracking
- [ ] **Release criteria**: Understand quality gates and release readiness requirements
- [ ] **Defect workflow**: Understand defect triage and resolution processes

### Project Manager Onboarding

- [ ] **Project history**: Review project timeline, key decisions, and current status
- [ ] **Risk register**: Review current risks and mitigation plans
- [ ] **Stakeholder relationships**: Understand stakeholder dynamics and history
- [ ] **Team dynamics**: Assess team health, velocity, and improvement areas
- [ ] **Pending decisions**: Identify outstanding decisions or blockers

### Product Manager Onboarding

- [ ] **Product vision**: Understand product strategy and roadmap
- [ ] **Customer insights**: Review customer research, feedback, and personas
- [ ] **Competitive landscape**: Understand market context and differentiators
- [ ] **Success metrics**: Review KPIs and measurement approach
- [ ] **Backlog strategy**: Understand prioritization framework and upcoming priorities

### Developer Onboarding

- [ ] **Codebase orientation**: Repository structure, architecture, and key patterns
- [ ] **Development environment**: Setup guide and local development configuration
- [ ] **Code standards**: Review coding conventions, linting, and review practices
- [ ] **CI/CD pipeline**: Understand build, test, and deployment processes
- [ ] **Technical debt**: Awareness of known issues and improvement areas

---

## Onboarding Verification

### Week 1 Check-in

- [ ] **Clarity confirmed**: Team member understands their role and responsibilities
- [ ] **Access verified**: All required access has been granted and tested
- [ ] **Introductions complete**: Key team members have been met
- [ ] **Questions addressed**: Initial questions have been answered
- [ ] **First deliverable**: Team member has made a first contribution or attended key meeting

### Week 2-4 Check-in

- [ ] **Productivity ramping**: Team member is contributing effectively
- [ ] **Process adherence**: Team member follows established processes
- [ ] **Integration complete**: Team member is fully integrated into team rituals
- [ ] **Feedback collected**: Gather feedback on onboarding experience for improvement

---

## Offboarding and Role Transition

When a team member transitions off a project:

- [ ] **Knowledge transfer**: Document and transfer key information
- [ ] **Handoff meetings**: Schedule handoff with replacement or team
- [ ] **Access revocation**: Remove access to project tools and repositories
- [ ] **Lessons learned**: Capture any insights for future onboarding improvements
- [ ] **Acknowledgment**: Thank the team member for their contributions
